Rachel Hedman, a college student at Chico State University, solved her problem of boredom with a new puppy. The Hedmans' quarantined puppy-raising experience has been similar to many others, with a "pandemic pets" phenomenon that has filled many new homes with dogs and cats. Lilli Castillo: On learning in a crowded home

By Molly Burke

March 21, 2020

With almost everyone working and learning online, it is easy to be distracted and feel cramped under one roof.

For Lilli Castillo, who is sheltering-in-place with 3 generations of family in Chicago, interruptions have made Zoom classes difficult. But Castillo sees the benefits of being with family during these trying times.

The Northwestern first-year student talks about what her quarantine looks like, with all its ups and downs.
